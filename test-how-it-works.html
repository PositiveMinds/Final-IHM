How HealthFlow Works - Carousel Test

Step 01

Connect Your Data

Link your patient database (Google Sheets, EMR, or Excel). HealthFlow reads your master patient list with all demographics, conditions, and metrics.

  • Auto-sync patient records
  • Multi-source integration
  • Real-time data mapping
Step 02

Configure Rules

Define patient triggers and alert conditions. Set custom rules for missed appointments, viral loads, medication adherence, and more.

  • Custom alert logic
  • Risk scoring
  • Automation workflows
Step 03

Automate & Scale

HealthFlow identifies at-risk patients, generates personalized messages, sends WhatsApp/email reminders, and delivers actionable reports.

  • AI-powered insights
  • Multi-channel messaging
  • Automated reporting

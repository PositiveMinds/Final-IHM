Step 1: Start by registering your health facilities. Each facility needs a unique facility ID and name.

Step 2: Register your patients with their demographic information, primary health conditions, and facility assignment.

Step 3: Schedule appointments for patients and track their appointment status throughout the system.

Step 4: Use the analytics dashboard to monitor patient outcomes, appointment compliance, and facility performance.

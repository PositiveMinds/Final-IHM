‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I configure the email service?

Call the configure method with your email provider credentials:

advancedChatbotFeatures.emailService.configure( 'sendgrid', // Provider: sendgrid, ses, smtp 'your-api-key', // API key from your provider 'noreply@health.local' // From email address );
Do I need to request notification permission?

Yes, browsers require explicit permission. Call this once:

await advancedChatbotFeatures.pushNotificationService.requestPermission();
What are the available recurring patterns?

Six patterns are available:

  • weekly - Every 1 week
  • bi-weekly - Every 2 weeks
  • monthly - Every 1 month
  • quarterly - Every 3 months
  • semi-annual - Every 6 months
  • annual - Every 1 year
What types of follow-ups are supported?

Five follow-up types are available:

  • post_appointment - 1 day after (email)
  • medication_reminder - 12 hours after (notification)
  • lab_results - 3 days after (email)
  • treatment_review - 7 days after (email)
  • quarterly_checkup - 90 days after (email)
How do I check for appointment conflicts?
const result = conflictDetector.checkConflicts(newAppointment, existingAppointments); if (result.hasConflicts) { result.conflicts.forEach(conflict => { console.log(conflict.message); }); }
